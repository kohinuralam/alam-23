Concept

Arandis Group focuses on fund data solutions through a technology-first approach – helping our clients find the best performance for their portfolio.

Our solutions are designed for Independent Financial Advisors, Wealth Managers, and Family Offices, with a primary focus on delivering data analytics of listed funds for advisor and client monitoring of investments.

Our data help identify opportunities for potential investments to improve portfolio returns.

Our technology-first approach means that we automate our workflows which allow for easier scaling and timely updates while removing opportunity for human error in traditional data collection processes.

We are also building a back-tested Machine Learning sample to identify the highest probability of over-performance within a given set of funds.

Our Solution

Arandis Group currently offers two solutions.

Via SaaS, we provide tools for fund comparisons and portfolio creation, which can be integrated directly into client sites, both internal and external. This delivers quick fund search and selection, customizable data views, as well as charting and comparisons. Charts can be rebased for accurate and detailed performance comparisons.

Via data feed, we provide our fund data directly to client databases, for client portfolios and reporting. Our data feed allows clients to build their own performance algorithm, and we can provide guidance and tools to help via our back-tested Machine Learning samples.

Our Data

Arandis Group has been collecting fund data from key markets in Europe. Since inception, we have grown to 50,000 daily funds processed, and we continue to expand to other major markets based on client demand.

Through our technology, all data is classified and captured in our proprietary Data Lake, ready for client consumption and analysis. This process also allows for dynamic data expansion, both in terms of breadth and depth.

The flexibility of our process also means that we can load full history for any fund and get it to clients within minutes, not hours or days.
